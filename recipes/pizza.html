Pizza

Description:

Delicious pizza has never been simpler with this classic pepperoni pizza recipe


Ingredients

  • 1 pound refrigerted pizza dough
  • 1/4 cup pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni

Steps

  1. Place pizza stone over a wood fire.
  2. Roll out the dough on a flat surface, place on the pizza stone and cook until golden on one side. About 10 minutes.
  3. Remove from the pizza stone. Spread the sauce evenly on the cooked side, leaving a bare crust edge. Sprinkle mozzarella and pepperoni on top of the sauce.
  4. Place back on the pizza stone and allow to cook for a further 10 minutes. Allow to cook slightly before cutting and serving.
